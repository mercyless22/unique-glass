We Care Your Dreams

At Unique Glass, we believe every space deserves a touch of brilliance. Established in 2018, we began our journey as a passionate team of creators focused on designing and manufacturing high-quality LED mirrors and custom glass solutions.

Over the years, we’ve grown from a small operation into a trusted name in the industry—thanks to our dedication to innovation, design, and quality craftsmanship.

Our work goes beyond just mirrors. Whether it’s elegant bathroom mirrors with built-in lighting, decorative glass panels, or practical installations for homes and businesses, we take pride in every detail. Every piece we create reflects not just light—but also our commitment to excellence.

To expand our capabilities and meet the evolving needs of our customers, we launched a sister company called Huzee. Through Huzee, we trade in advanced digital locks—helping modernize home and office security—and also manufacture strainer filters, which are essential in various industrial applications.

This combination of manufacturing and trading allows us to offer a wide range of products that balance both aesthetic value and technical performance.
